# Jess — Quick Start

Jess is your voice-first ADHD assistant. Talk to her through the WebUI or say "Hey Jess" on any ATOM Echo speaker.

## Focus & Productivity

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "Start a focus timer for 25 minutes on emails" | Pomodoro timer with ambient audio, site blocking, and check-ins |
| "Start a body doubling session, 3 sprints" | Multi-sprint focus with breaks between each |
| "Stop focus" / "I'm done" | Ends the timer early |
| "Next sprint" / "Extend by 10 minutes" | Continue or add time during a session |
| "Break down cleaning the kitchen" | Splits into micro-steps with time estimates |
| "Done" / "Skip" / "Next step" | Advance through decomposed task steps |

## Routines

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "Start morning routine" | Step-by-step guidance: meds → shower → breakfast → calendar check |
| "Start evening routine" | Wind-down: evening meds → tomorrow's calendar → screens away |
| "Done" / "Skip" / "Pause routine" | Control the flow — Jess waits for you, nudges if you go quiet |

Routines also auto-trigger at 7:00 AM and 9:00 PM.

## Brain Dumps

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "Brain dump: call the dentist, buy groceries, research flights" | Jess categorizes each item and routes it to memory or reminders |

## Reminders & Calendar

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "Remind me to call Mom at 3pm" | TTS announcement at 3pm + phone notification |
| "What's on my calendar?" | Today's events read aloud |
| "Add pickleball Thursday at 7pm" | Creates a Google Calendar event |

Jess also proactively announces upcoming events with escalating alerts (1hr → 30min → 15min → 5min) and "leave by" times for events with physical locations.

## Self-Care

Jess monitors meals, meds, water, and movement — nudging you when something is overdue:

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "I had lunch" / "Just ate a sandwich" | Logs your meal, stops food nudges |
| "I took my meds" / "Yes I took it" | Logs medication, tells you next dose |
| "Had some water" | Resets hydration timer |

You don't need to do anything — nudges come to you via speakers every 15 minutes if something is overdue.

## Interruptions

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "I need to take this call" / "BRB" | Bookmarks what you were doing, checks in after 5 minutes |
| "What was I working on?" | Lists your last 3 activities with timestamps |

## Smart Home

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "Turn off the bedroom lights" | Controls any HA device |
| "Set the office fan to 50%" | Brightness, temperature, scenes |

## Decision Help

| Say this | What happens |
|----------|-------------|
| "What should I work on?" | One directive recommendation based on your tasks and calendar |
| "I'm overwhelmed" | Triage mode: single most important thing, everything else dismissed |
| "What should I eat?" | Two quick options based on your preferences |

## Proactive Features (automatic)

| Feature | When |
|---------|------|
| Morning briefing | 7:00 AM — today's events + pending reminders |
| Calendar alerts | Tiered countdown: 1hr, 30min, 15min, 5min before events |
| Email alerts | Every 30 min — new Primary inbox emails |
| Daily progress recap | 6:00 PM — tasks done, focus time, streaks |
| Weekly digest | Sunday 7:00 PM — week summary with trends |
| Ambient summaries | 10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm — brief status check |
| Self-care nudges | Every 15 min — meals, meds, water, movement (if overdue) |

## Tips

- **One thing at a time.** Jess never gives you a list when one answer will do.
- **No guilt.** Skip anything. Miss a routine. Jess never makes you feel bad.
- **You don't need to check anything.** Everything important comes to you via speakers.
- **"Hey Jess" works from any room** with an ATOM Echo speaker.
