# Jess — Reference Card

*When your brain needs help remembering what Jess can help with. Grouped by how you feel, not by feature. Read top-to-bottom; each row works on its own.*

---

## I can't start

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"Start a 25 minute focus on [thing]"** | Pomodoro + ambient audio + site blocking |
| **"Start a body doubling session, 3 sprints"** | Multi-sprint focus with breaks |
| **"Break down [big task]"** | Splits into tiny steps with time estimates |
| **"What should I work on?"** | One directive pick — no list |

## I can't stop thinking about stuff

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"Brain dump: [everything in your head]"** | Sorts each item into memory or reminders |
| **"Remind me to [thing] at [time]"** | TTS + phone notification at that time |
| **"Remember that [fact about me]"** | Writes it into your memory palace |

## I lost my place

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"What was I working on?"** | Last 3 activities with timestamps |
| **"BRB"** or **"I need to take this call"** | Bookmarks context, checks in after 5 min |
| **"Done"** or **"Next step"** | Advances through a task you're decomposing |

## I'm overwhelmed

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"I'm overwhelmed"** | Triage: single most important thing, rest dismissed |
| **"What should I eat?"** | Two options, no more |
| **"Goodnight"** | DND — silences everything until morning briefing |

## Body stuff

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"I had lunch"** / **"Just ate"** | Logs meal, stops food nudges |
| **"I took my meds"** | Logs meds, tells you next dose |
| **"Had some water"** | Resets hydration timer |
| *(do nothing)* | Jess nudges every ~15 min if something's overdue |

## House stuff

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"Turn off the bedroom lights"** | Any HA device |
| **"Set the office fan to 50%"** | Brightness, scenes, temperature |
| **"Add milk to my grocery list"** | Shopping list (multiple lists supported) |
| **"What's on my grocery list?"** | Reads it back |

## Calendar & email

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"What's on my calendar?"** | Today's events read aloud |
| **"Add pickleball Thursday at 7pm"** | Creates a Google Calendar event |
| **"Any new emails?"** | Read-only check of Primary inbox |

## Routines

| Say this | What Jess does |
|---|---|
| **"Start morning routine"** | Step-by-step: meds → shower → breakfast → calendar |
| **"Start evening routine"** | Wind-down: meds → tomorrow's calendar → screens away |
| *(do nothing)* | Routines auto-trigger at 7am and 9pm |

## Things Jess does without you asking

- **7am** — Morning briefing (events, reminders, weather, yesterday's parked item)
- **Before every event** — Tiered alerts (1hr, 30min, 15min, 5min) + "leave by" for drives
- **10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm** — Ambient status summaries
- **Every 15 min (if overdue)** — Self-care nudges
- **6pm** — Daily progress recap
- **9:30pm** — Evening shutdown ritual (tomorrow's first event + leave-by, meds check, parks one unfinished thing for the morning)
- **Sunday 7pm** — Weekly digest
- **10 min after chats** — Auto-learn facts from conversation → encrypted memory

Or say **"Goodnight"** and deal with it tomorrow.
