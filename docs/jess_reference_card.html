Jess — Reference Card When your brain needs a nudge. Grouped by how you feel, not by feature. Each row works on its own.

I can't start

  • "Start a 25 minute focus on [thing]"
    Pomodoro + ambient audio + site blocking
  • "Start a body doubling session, 3 sprints"
    Multi-sprint focus with breaks
  • "Break down [big task]"
    Tiny steps with time estimates
  • "What should I work on?"
    One directive pick — no list

Can't stop thinking

  • "Brain dump: [everything]"
    Sorts each item into memory or reminder
  • "Remind me to [X] at [time]"
    TTS + phone notification
  • "Remember that [fact about me]"
    Writes to your memory palace

I lost my place

  • "What was I working on?"
    Last 3 activities with timestamps
  • "BRB" / "Need to take this call"
    Bookmarks context, checks in after 5 min
  • "Done" / "Next step" / "Skip"
    Advance through a decomposed task

I'm overwhelmed

  • "I'm overwhelmed"
    Triage: single most important thing
  • "What should I eat?"
    Two options, no more
  • "Goodnight"
    DND until morning
